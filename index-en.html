What We Do

We purchase food and FMCG products directly from Italian manufacturers and redistribute them to alternative markets and the third sector, without interfering with the traditional sales networks of our suppliers.

Over the years, we've also specialized in fresh goods and the non-food sector, such as cleaning products and personal hygiene items (toiletries).

Sustainability

We are committed to reducing waste and promoting resource circularity. We collaborate with numerous non-profits and charitable organizations by offering products at reduced costs to support their solidarity initiatives.

Our warehouses are equipped with solar panels and we use green energy to power our logistics operations.
